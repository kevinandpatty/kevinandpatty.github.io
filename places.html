PLACES TO SEE

Harry's Chocolate Shop
Boisterous, campus-adjacent pub serving American bar fare in a vintage setting since 1919. Kevin took Patty to Harry’s on her 21st birthday, the day before her organic chemistry final.
Address: 329 W State St, West Lafayette, IN 47906

Engineering Fountain
The Purdue Engineering Mall Water Sculpture. Patty led many new students through their first “fountain run” during BGR.
610 Purdue Mall, West Lafayette, IN 47906

Triple XXX Family Restaurant
Old-school institution for burgers, root beer & other soda-fountain treats, plus breakfast all day. As on Guy Fieri’s Diners, Drive-ins and Dives.
Address: 2 N Salisbury St, West Lafayette, IN 47906

France A. Córdova Recreational Sports Center
Recreation center of Purdue University. Kevin used to put up legendary rebound numbers at the rec during his time at Purdue.
Address: 355 N Martin Jischke Dr, West Lafayette, IN 47906

Nine Irish Brothers
Classic Irish pub with a sizable whiskey list offers hearty meals, bar bites & rollicking music. Kevin also took Patty here for her 21st Birthday.
Address: 119 Howard Ave, West Lafayette, IN 47906

Birck Boilermaker Golf Complex
We are lucky enough to have two golf courses at Purdue. The Pete Dye designed Kampen Course hosted the 2008 NCAA Men's Championships and the 2003 NCAA Women's Championships. A friend once described it as “deceiving.”
Address: 1300 Cherry Ln, West Lafayette, IN 47907
